# 🧠 Product Spec: The Google AI Studio Advantage

**Core Thesis:** In the indie arbitrage economy, **Runway is King**. We choose Google AI Studio not just for its tech, but for its zero-cost operational model.

---

## 1. The Financial Arbitrage ($0 vs $$$)
*   **The Gap**: Platforms like Cursor, Claude Pro, or ChatGPT Plus cost money ($20-$40/mo) and have strict message caps.
*   **The Advantage**: Google AI Studio provides a massive free tier for development. You can iterate 100 times a day without hitting a paywall.
*   **Strategy**: Spend your money on **traffic and ads**, not on AI subscriptions.

## 2. Visual Alchemy (The Vision Kill-Shot)
Gemini 2.0/3.0 features world-leading vision capabilities integrated into the AI Studio interface.
*   **Sketch-to-Product**: Draw a rough idea on a napkin -> Snap a photo -> Generate React structure.
*   **Design Theft (Ethical)**: Upload a screenshot of a site you love -> "Extract the layout and semantic tokens" -> Apply to your scaffold.
*   **Comparison**: AI Studio's ability to maintain "Design Intent" from an image currently exceeds paid competitors.

## 3. From "Messy Thoughts" to Architecture
AI Studio's long-context window (1M+ tokens) allows you to dump a massive, unorganized "brain dump" of an idea.
*   **The Scaffold's Role**: Gemini writes the "Ambitious Brain". Instant Ship™ provides the "Hardened Body" (Routing, SEO, Deployment) to contain it safely.

## 4. The Native Workflow
*   **Constraint**: AI Studio runs in the browser.
*   **Solution**: Our scaffold handles the **blob URL origin** issues and **importmap conflicts** that usually kill AI Studio previews, enabling a "Prompt & Play" loop.
