# 📈 Growth Spec: Advanced SEO Rules (Combat Tactics)

**Goal:** Breaking into the Top 3 Ranking Positions.
**Formula:** `Rank = (Content + Backlinks + Tech) * User Interaction`

---

## 1. The Revenue Funnel
**Principle:** You must answer: "How much MRR did moving from Rank #5 to #3 generate?"
*   **Action:** Track full funnel: Ranking -> Click -> Visit -> Signup -> Paid.

## 2. User Interaction (The "Last Click" Rule)
**Principle:** Google wants the user to stop searching after visiting your page.
*   **Goal:** Become the **Last Click**.
*   **Tactics:**
    *   **Depth:** Your content must be more comprehensive than the Top 10 combined.
    *   **Assets:** Provide downloadable templates or tools to increase Dwell Time.
    *   **Exit Path:** Clear "Next Steps" (CTA) to keep them in your ecosystem, not back to Google.

## 3. The Ranking Ceilings
*   **Top 30 (Entry Ticket):** Determined by **Content Quality** & **Technical SEO** (Speed, Schema).
*   **Top 8 (Front Page):** Determined by **Backlinks** (Authority).
*   **Top 3 (The King):** Determined by **User Interaction** (CTR, Dwell Time, Bounce Rate).

## 4. Backlink Strategy: Precision Strikes
*   **Guest Posts:** Best for promoting high-intent **Product Pages**, not generic blogs.
*   **Diversity:** Mix your link profile (Guest posts, HARO, Directories, Forums).
*   **Highest ROI Zone:** Focus link building on pages ranking **#10 - #25**. They have the foundation but need the "Authority Push" to hit the front page.
