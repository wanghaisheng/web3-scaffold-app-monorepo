# 📢 Growth Spec: AI Marketing Prompts & Frameworks

**Objective:** Use these prompts to generate high-conversion copy and marketing assets that align with the brand philosophy.

---

## 1. Landing Page Optimization Prompt
**Goal:** Refactor a landing page to drive emotional connection and trust.

**Prompt:**
> "Act as a Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO) Expert.
> **Product:** [Product Name] - [One sentence pitch].
> **Target Audience:** [Persona].
>
> **Task:** Refactor the Landing Page copy using the **'Logic + Emotion' Dual Drive** framework.
>
> **Requirements:**
> 1.  **Identity Projection:** Define who the user *becomes* by using this (e.g., 'Join the Elite').
> 2.  **Emotional Curve:**
>     *   *Empathy:* Validate their current pain (The 'Maintenance Trap').
>     *   *Catharsis:* Describe the relief of the solution (The '2-Hour Ritual').
> 3.  **Fear to Safety:** Shift from 'FOMO' to 'Psychological Safety' (e.g., 'Guaranteed Results').
> 4.  **Trust Stack:** Add 'Vulnerability' (admit what the tool *can't* do) to build deep trust.
> 5.  **Aha! Moment:** Describe the exact moment the user feels 'This is it'."

---

## 2. Multi-Channel Content Generation Prompt
**Goal:** repurpose core content for different platforms.

**Prompt:**
> "You are a Solo Entrepreneur building in public.
> **Source Content:** [Paste PRD or Blog Post].
>
> **Task:** Generate 4 unique pieces of content for the following channels:
>
> 1.  **Twitter/X (Thread):** Hooky, fast-paced, focuses on the 'Aha!' moment. Use bullet points.
> 2.  **LinkedIn (Thought Leadership):** Professional tone. Focus on the 'Business Impact' and 'Efficiency Arbitrage'.
> 3.  **Indie Hackers (Story):** Vulnerable, narrative-driven. Focus on the 'Struggle' and the 'Breakthrough'.
> 4.  **WeChat/Little Red Book (Chinese):** Cultural adaptation. Focus on 'Techniques', 'Dry Goods' (干货), and emotional resonance.
>
> **Constraint:** Maintain the brand voice: 'Confident, Experienced, Anti-Mainstream'."

---

## 3. The "Product-Symbol-Emotion" Check
Before publishing, ask:
*   Does this copy mention the **Product** (Features)?
*   Does it establish a **Symbol** (Visual/Narrative Identity)?
*   Does it promise an **Emotional Outcome** (Relief, Power, Belonging)?
