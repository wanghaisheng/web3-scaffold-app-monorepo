# 🤖 Growth Spec: AI Content Generation SOP

**Objective:** AI as Co-Pilot. Human as Pilot. Efficiency up, Quality stable.

---

## The 6-Step Protocol

### 1. 【Discovery】 Keyword & Intent (15m)
*   **Action**: Use Ahrefs/Semrush to find 1 Core + 5 Long-tail keywords.
*   **Validation**: Check Google Top 10. (Are they blogs? Tools? Guides?). Align format with intent.

### 2. 【Structure】 The "Frankenstein" Outline (10m)
*   **Action**: Extract H2/H3s from the Top 5 competitors.
*   **Synthesis**: Merge them into a "Super Outline" that covers everything they do, plus what they missed.

### 3. 【Mapping】 Keyword Injection (5m)
*   **Action**: Assign keywords to specific H2/H3 headers.

### 4. 【Input】 Context Injection (5m)
*   **Action**: Prepare the "Context Pack" for the AI.
    *   Brand Voice.
    *   Specific Product Features/Pricing.
    *   Unique Data/Case Studies (AI cannot invent these).

### 5. 【Generation】 Section-by-Section Prompting (15m)
*   **Rule**: Never generate the whole article at once.
*   **Prompt**: "Write the section for [H2 Header]. Include keywords [X, Y]. Use [Context Data]."

### 6. 【Refinement】 The Human Touch (10m)
*   **Fact Check**: Verify all claims.
*   **Visuals**: Insert screenshots/diagrams.
*   **Internal Links**: Add 5-10 links to other cluster pages.
*   **Tone Check**: Ensure it sounds like *you*, not a robot.
