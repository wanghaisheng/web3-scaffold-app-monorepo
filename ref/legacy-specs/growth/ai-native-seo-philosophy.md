# 🧠 Growth Spec: AI-Native SEO Philosophy

**Core Thesis:** In the era of AI Overviews and Chatbots, the battleground of SEO has shifted from "Keyword Rankings" to "**AI Citation Authority**". Our goal is to become the "Primary Fact Source" that models trust and cite.

---

## 1. The 3 Core Philosophies

### A. SEO is Foundational Hygiene, Not Magic
*   **Old View:** Tricks to fool Google.
*   **New View:** **Making information parseable.**
*   **Tactics:**
    *   **Structure:** `JSON-LD` and `Schema.org` are mandatory. They translate prose into structured data (Entities, Attributes, Relationships).
    *   **Crawlability:** `llms.txt` is the new `robots.txt`. It is the "Official API" for crawlers.
    *   **Cross-Referencing:** Dense internal linking helps AI understand your internal knowledge graph.

### B. AI Mentions Follow External Signals
*   **Old View:** Good content brings traffic.
*   **New View:** **AI answers reflect the consensus of the web.**
*   **Implication:** Off-Page SEO is more critical. High-quality backlinks, Reddit discussions, and media mentions vote for your brand's authority in the AI's "Cognitive World".

### C. Measure Conversion, Not Impressions
*   **Old View:** Tracking "Ranking Position".
*   **New View:** **Tracking "Referral Traffic" from AI.**
*   **Metric:** Stop counting how many times ChatGPT mentions you. Start counting how many users click through from an AI answer and convert.

---

## 2. Four Actionable Strategies

1.  **Help Center Injection**: Structure FAQ and Help Center content with `FAQPage` and `HowTo` schema. These specific, long-tail answers are AI catnip.
2.  **Internal Q&A Knowledge Base**: Standardize answers to common industry questions (from Reddit/Quora) and publish them.
3.  **Chatbot Referral Monitoring**: Watch "Referral" traffic in GA4 for sources like `chatgpt.com`, `gemini.google.com`.
4.  **Citation Source Targeting**: Identify which sites AI models cite most often (e.g., specific tech blogs) and target them for guest posts.
