
# 🦁 Growth Spec: Google Content Quality Protocol (GCQ)

**Objective:** To strictly adhere to Google's "Helpful, Reliable, People-First" content standards.

---

## 1. The Core Philosophy: "People-First"
**The Golden Rule:** Content must be created primarily to help people, not to manipulate search rankings.

| ✅ People-First (Do This) | ❌ Search Engine-First (Avoid This) |
| :--- | :--- |
| Writing for an existing, intended audience. | Creating content on "Trending Topics" outside your niche. |
| Demonstrating first-hand expertise and depth. | Summarizing what others say without adding value. |

---

## 2. The "Who, How, Why" Transparency Framework

### A. WHO (Created this?)
*   **Requirement:** It must be self-evident to visitors who authored the content.
*   **Action Items:**
    *   **Bylines:** Every post must have an author name (`By [Author Name]`).
    *   **Author Page:** A dedicated bio page detailing background and credentials.

### B. HOW (Was it created?)
*   **Requirement:** Readers want to know the effort and methodology.
*   **Action Items:**
    *   **Disclosure:** If AI was used for drafting, disclose it (e.g., "Assisted by AI, Verified by [Role]").
    *   **Evidence:** Show proof of work (Original screenshots, photos, or data logs).

---

## 3. E-E-A-T Compliance (The Trust Metric)

1.  **Experience**: Prove you actually used the product or method. Use "In my testing..."
2.  **Expertise**: Link to professional profiles (GitHub/LinkedIn). Use industry-standard terminology.
3.  **Authoritativeness**: Stick to your niche to build topical authority.
4.  **Trustworthiness**: Use HTTPS, cite external sources, and maintain factual accuracy.

---

## 4. The Value Add Rule
If the information can be found identically on Wikipedia or via a basic AI query, the page lacks "Value Add". 
**Every page must provide:**
- Unique organization or curation.
- Personal perspective or analysis.
- Interactive elements (Calculators, Checklists).
