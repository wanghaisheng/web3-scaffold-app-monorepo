# 🚀 Growth Spec: Off-Page SEO Protocol (Module D)

**Objective:** Build Domain Authority (DA) through value-driven outreach.

---

## Phase 1: Intelligence (The Setup)
*   **Target List**: Identify 50-100 websites with DA > 40 in your niche.
*   **Monitoring**: Set up Google Alerts for your brand name.
*   **Link Magnets**: Identify 2-3 pieces of content (Original Research, Ultimate Guides) worth linking to.

## Phase 2: Proactive Outreach (The Hustle)
*   **HARO / Qwoted**: Respond to reporter queries daily.
    *   *KPI*: 10+ responses/month.
*   **Guest Posting**: Pitch unique value angles to target blogs.
    *   *KPI*: 5+ pitches/month.
*   **Directories**: Submit to G2, Capterra, Product Hunt (One-time).

## Phase 3: Reactive & Community (The Defense)
*   **Unlinked Mentions**: Detect brand mentions without links -> Contact author -> Request link.
    *   *KPI*: 100% reclamation rate within 7 days.
*   **Community Value**: Answer questions on Reddit/Quora. Link to your solution ONLY when it solves the specific problem.

## Phase 4: Auditing (The Scorecard)
*   **Monthly**: Track new backlinks via Ahrefs/Semrush.
*   **Quarterly**: Disavow toxic links to protect domain health.
