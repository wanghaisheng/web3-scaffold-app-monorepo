# 🤖 Growth Spec: Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)

**Goal:** Become the "Citation Source" for AI Models (Gemini, ChatGPT).

## 1. The 3 Core Philosophies
1.  **Structure is King**: Use JSON-LD and Semantic HTML. AI reads structure better than prose.
2.  **External Signals**: AI mentions follow external authority (Backlinks, Reddit discussions).
3.  **Conversion over Impressions**: Measure traffic from "Referral: AI", not just rankings.

## 2. Implementation Strategy
*   **`llms.txt`**: A markdown map explicitly for crawlers, generated at build time.
*   **Deep Schema**: Use nested JSON-LD (Author -> Organization -> Article) to establish E-E-A-T.
*   **Direct Answers**: Start sections with concise definitions ("X is Y") to win Featured Snippets and AI summaries.
