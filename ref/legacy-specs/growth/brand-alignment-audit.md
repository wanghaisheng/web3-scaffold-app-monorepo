# ⚖️ Growth Spec: Brand Alignment Audit

## 1. The Core Narrative
**"Visuals by Drawing (G), Logic by Speaking (L), Launch Globally (C)."**

## 2. The "Missing L" Problem
**Observation**: Many early marketing materials focused heavily on AI Studio (G) and Cloudflare (C) but neglected Lovable (L).
**Impact**: Users doubted the "Fullstack" claim because the bridge between UI and Production was missing.
**Correction**: We must explicitly mention **"Logic Injection"** or **"Backend by Voice"** in all copy to close the loop.

## 3. Audit Checklist for New Content
Before publishing any new marketing material or documentation, verify:

- [ ] **The Trinity**: Does it mention all three pillars (G+L+C)?
- [ ] **The Villain**: Does it identify the "Maintenance Trap" or "Localhost Hell"?
- [ ] **The Hero**: Is the user the "Architect/Commander", not just a coder?
- [ ] **The Outcome**: Is the result a "Shipped Asset", not just a "Prototype"?

## 4. Consistency in Terminology
-   **Don't say**: "Coding Tool" -> **Say**: "Shipping Engine".
-   **Don't say**: "Template" -> **Say**: "Protocol".
-   **Don't say**: "Fast Website" -> **Say**: "High-Authority Asset".
