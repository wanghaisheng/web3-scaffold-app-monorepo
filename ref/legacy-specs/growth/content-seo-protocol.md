# 🔄 Growth Spec: Content SEO & Maintenance (Module E)

**Objective:** Treat content as a living asset to prevent decay.

---

## Phase 1: Strategic Planning
*   **Cadence**: 2-3 High-quality pieces per week.
*   **Mix**: Balance TOFU (Educational), MOFU (Comparison), BOFU (Transactional).

## Phase 2: Money Page Creation
**Goal**: Capture high-intent traffic.
*   **Comparison Pages**: `X vs Y`.
*   **Alternative Pages**: `Best Alternative to X`.
*   **Use Case Pages**: `[Product] for [Role]`.

## Phase 3: The Evergreen Engine (Maintenance)
**Goal**: Combat "Content Decay".
*   **Cycle**: Audit content every 6 months.
*   **Triggers**: Traffic drop > 10% or outdated year in title.
*   **Action**:
    1.  Update stats/dates.
    2.  Add new sections based on competitor analysis.
    3.  Update "Last Updated" date.
    4.  Request Re-indexing in GSC.

## Phase 4: Amplification (Repurposing)
**Rule**: One creation, infinite distribution.
*   **Blog Post** -> Twitter Thread (Summary).
*   **Blog Post** -> LinkedIn Article (Business Angle).
*   **Blog Post** -> Newsletter (Teaser).
