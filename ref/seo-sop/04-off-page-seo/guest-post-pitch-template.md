## 📝 Pitch邮件模板库

### 模板1: 标准Guest Post Pitch

**适用场景**: 首次联系，正式请求投稿机会

```
Subject: Guest Post Idea: [Specific Topic] for [Site Name]

Hi [Editor Name],

I'm [Your Name], [Title] at China CT Scanner, a platform dedicated to Chinese medical imaging equipment information for international healthcare professionals.

I've been following [Site Name] and particularly enjoyed your recent article on [specific article title]. It resonated with trends I'm seeing in the medical imaging sector.

I'd love to contribute a guest article to your site. Here are a few topic ideas that might interest your readers:

1. **[Topic 1]**: [One-sentence description + why it's relevant]
2. **[Topic 2]**: [One-sentence description + why it's relevant]
3. **[Topic 3]**: [One-sentence description + why it's relevant]

A bit about me:
- [Years] years in medical equipment industry
- [Credential or achievement]
- Previously published on [Publication names if applicable]

I'm happy to adjust these topics or propose something new based on your editorial needs.

Looking forward to hearing from you.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
[Title], China CT Scanner
chinactscanner.org
[LinkedIn profile - optional]
```

## ✅ Pitch最佳实践

### 发送前检查清单

```markdown
- [ ] 个性化：包含编辑姓名和站点具体信息
- [ ] 相关性：参考了站点近期文章
- [ ] 价值：明确说明读者将获得什么
- [ ] 简洁：邮件在2分钟内可读完
- [ ] 专业：无语法错误，格式整洁
- [ ] CTA：明确的下一步（等待回复/发送大纲）
```

### 时间策略

```
最佳发送时间:
- 周二至周四
- 上午9-11点（目标时区）
- 避免周一早晨和周五下午

跟进节奏:
- 第1封后7天发跟进
- 第2封后14天可再次尝试
- 最多3次联系，之后放弃
```

### 内容指南

```
Guest Post内容要求:
- 字数: 1,500-2,500字
- 原创: 100%未发布内容
- 格式: 包含H2/H3、列表、数据
- 链接: 1-2个回链（正文内自然植入）
- 图片: 提供2-3张相关图片
```
